Welcome to Eclipse Builds LLC!

We take pride in serving the best PC repair and computer building services in the world. We are a family owned and operated business that has been serving the community for over 30 years. We are located in the heart of the city of Charlotte, North Carolina. Our customers come to us for all of their computing needs and we are always ready to help you.


Our Founder

Our founder, Abu Marzouq, is a computer science student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. He is a graduate of the University of North Carolina at Charlotte and has been working in the industry for the past 10 years. He has worked in the computer industry for many different companies and has been a part of many different teams. He has worked on many different projects and has been able to work on many different projects. He is our inspiration and what drives us to serve our customers the very best every day.
